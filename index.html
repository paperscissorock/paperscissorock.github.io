WHO?

Hi! I'm Kevin. I grew up in Texas fueled by tacos, fútbol, skateboarding and all things visual. My design addiction started with either my childhood matchbook or stamp collection. I used to stick to static 2D graphics but I'm now enjoying learning to give life and movement to them. I've always enjoyed using my hands to build and create objects and worlds, in both reality and in digital realms. I love to design in the latter, then build. When I'm not designing you can probably find me: outside, tinkering, making music, making photographs, gaming, reading or spending time with my people and/or animals.

After graduating college and a short stint entertaining a medical career, I moved overseas to Krakow, Poland and re-immersed myself in design. I worked at a small design firm, taught English, and worked part-time at a bike shop. I lived out my two-year residency and afforded myself the opportunity to travel extensively while growing my skill set.

Fast forward to today and I've been living in Portland, Oregon for over three years, freelancing seven while working other part-time jobs, and enthusiastically learning new skills to take the next step - a full time position on a creative team producing visual content.

Here live my credentials.
