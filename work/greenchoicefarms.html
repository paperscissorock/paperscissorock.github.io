Recipe

Green Choice Farms has been a great client to get familiar with an emerging industry. I have worked with the fine engineers who envisioned Green Choice on more than one business venture. I was contracted to develop the brand identity and online presence starting with a logo to help stand out in an exponentially growing industry flooded with very similar identity elements. It was a challenge, but I feel that I've been successful at driving the company message and have gained recognition from industry leaders. One of my favorite parts of the logo I developed was hiding an energy bolt in the negative space to further represent their dedication to energy efficiency.

This has been a great project to work on that has allowed me a lot of creative freedom. It's provided room to grow and learn more software and ways to get get my message across as well as the opportunity to learn about a growing industry.
