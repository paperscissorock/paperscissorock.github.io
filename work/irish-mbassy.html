This was a client I worked with while at wildebydesign. I was tasked with designing and maintaining their website and updating their event calendar and weekly advertisements. I also did some print work for them during the 2014 World Cup, which was great to combine two passions into one thing.

This was my first Muse website and a great project to work on. I learned a new piece of software, soon to be outdated and replaced by something else to learn. It turns out the local Irish Pub was not only a great client and place to grab a pint but also the ideal environment to watch those World Cup games at!
