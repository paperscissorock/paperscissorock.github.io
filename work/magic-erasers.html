A stop motion project I worked on as part of my Art Direction 1 class at Texas State University. We received the brief and client and went to work on our concept and execution. The concept was based loosely around 2001 : A Space Odyssey and stop motion animation was the decided storytelling medium. I was mainly for the entire set creation, a majority of the photography/filming, Photoshop prepress work and animations. This was one of my favorite projects to work on.

• Published in Graphis’ 2007 Journal
• TX State University Ralph Award 2007
• TX State University Medal of Excellence 2007
